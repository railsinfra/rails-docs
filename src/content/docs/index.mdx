<p class="rails-intro__body">
  <strong>Rails</strong> is banking infrastructure: identity, accounts, and a double-entry ledger behind a
  single gateway. You use it when you need programmatic control over users, balances, and financial
  finality—without folding those concerns into one database or one deployable.
</p>
